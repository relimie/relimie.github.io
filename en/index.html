Drink on Your Own Terms

Relimie isn't just about quitting drinking; it's a companion for those who enjoy a drink but want to prevent their habits from becoming an addiction — without the guilt or judgment that often comes with tracking.

In Relimie, everything revolves around your Personal Baseline (which is why we call our method "baseline-drinking"). Instead of a rigid daily limit that feels like a failure if you miss it, your baseline is a long-term anchor. Life happens: some nights you'll have an extra glass, others you won't drink at all. What matters is the trend. Over time, your habits naturally gravitate back toward your goal.

Tapping your glowing Orb shows you where you stand at a glance: Green — you're comfortably within your baseline; Gold — you're slightly over your limit; Red — you should consider pausing for a few days to bring your average back in line. The green signs also show you how many drinks you have avoided so far. You can use this information to adjust your baseline down and reduce your consumption step by step, if you wish.

To help you choose your baseline, we provide data from WHO recommendations and average values for your country and gender. These serve as orientation until you find your personal values. Relimie also supports a variety of measurement units, so you can use those you understand best (like grams of pure alcohol or standard US units).

Keep in mind that while baseline-drinking is a powerful helper for controlling your behavior, it won't do the work for you. You need to put in the effort to stick to your baseline and log accurately. Since the baseline is based on aggregation, it needs some time to "kick in" as it collects data. Don't be discouraged if you don't see it change immediately, even though it becomes available right after onboarding.

Connect the Dots: Why You Drink

Tracking numbers is just the start. The Diary is where you find the why.

By logging how you feel — before a drink, the morning after, or on a clear-headed day — you start to see the patterns that usually stay hidden. It's about spotting triggers and finding a way to stay ahead of them. The diary helps you understand the consequences and spot the moments when you need to take control.

Contextual tags appear when you need them:

  • Triggers surface when you log alcohol (stress, boredom, social pressure)
  • After-Effects wait for you the next morning (hangover, anxiety, clarity)
  • Sober Feelings highlight the wins (productivity, pride, energy)

Inside the Analytics Hub, your most frequent tags are ranked against your drinking days — helping you anticipate the situations where you're most likely to slip, so you can stay ahead of them. And, of course, you can add your own tags in the settings menu.

Logging without the Friction

Tracking shouldn't be a chore. Relimie is built to get you in and out in seconds, while automatically capturing the details that actually matter.

Every entry adds up to your big picture:

  • Alcohol — Track the total pure alcohol consumed with each drink.
  • Spending — See exactly what you're spending. Prices are preset based on your country's averages, but you can always adjust them for a single drink or the whole day. Just long-press a drink in the list or the total amount at the bottom. Prices are also split between "home" and "outside" consumption for better accuracy.
  • Calories — Each drink comes with predefined calorie counts so you can see the real impact on your fitness goals, not just the unit count. If you need to be exact, you can adjust these for any drink.

Relimie features a catalog of the most common drinks, and you can easily add your own. To make adding new drinks as simple as possible, use the AI Sparkle button. It appears in the search bar whenever you enter a drink not found in the catalog. One tap will research and autofill the ABV and calories, so you never have to guess. Feel free to provide feedback if something doesn't work as expected — we’re always improving.

All of this data flows into your Analytics Hub, where daily entries become the insights that help you stay in control.

🎁 And most importantly: Logging your alcohol, calories, and spending is completely free, along with the essential analytics to track them. You don't need a premium subscription to use these features for as long as you like!

See Your Story. Own Your Data.

The Analytics Hub turns your daily entries into a clear picture of your balance. Just swipe up from the Home Screen to see where you stand.

What's inside:

  • History — Go back and forth in time.
  • Overview — Your spending, caloric impact, and the ratio of clear days to drinking ones (available in the free version as well).
  • Habits — A graphical overview of your drinking habits.
  • Trends — Spot the specific days and times when you're most likely to drift off course.
  • Log — Look back at any date to adjust entries, update notes, or refine how you felt.

Private by Design:

Your data never leaves your device. When you want to, you can generate a "Share My Story" PDF to show progress to someone — without sharing your private journal notes. Or export a JSON Backup for your own analysis.

No cloud, no tracking, no third-party access. Ever.

Cravings Breaker

Breathe Through the Impulse

A craving doesn't ask for permission. It hits fast, and in those first 30 seconds, willpower alone can feel like it's not enough. But if you can disrupt that initial impulse, you give your brain the space it needs to step back and stay on track.

Your Instant Circuit Breaker

Long-press the Orb on the Home Screen to trigger the Breaker immediately. Your screen transforms into a calm, pulsing guide for the 4-7-8 Breathing Technique. As you breathe, grounding quotes help anchor your focus and steady your mind until the urge passes.

Turn a Challenge into a Win

When the cycle ends, Relimie prompts you to log a Clear Day. By turning a moment of resistance into a trackable achievement, you transform a difficult impulse into another brick in your streak. Every urge you ride out is proof of your progress.
