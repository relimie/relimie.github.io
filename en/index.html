Relimie v2.0.0

Enjoy the moment without losing your edge.

Relimie is the mindful drinking companion for self-confident individuals who value clarity and control. This is not an app designed to force you to quit; it is a tool for finding your own balance. We believe you don’t need advice, judgment, or preaching—you need the facts to draw your own conclusions.

A New Way to Look at Limits

Most trackers treat your goals as a "pass or fail" daily test, but life is rarely that linear. At the heart of Relimie is the Long-Term Baseline, a unique approach to moderation that focuses on your overall trend rather than a single evening. Your limit isn't a rigid wall; it’s a guide. If you have a heavy night, the app doesn’t reset a clock or shame you. Instead, it shows you exactly how your long-term balance is affected and helps you visualize the path back to your personal baseline. Whether you are looking to lower your intake or simply want to stay aware, Relimie gives you the perspective to stay in the driver’s seat.

The Orb: Your Mindful Interruption

The centerpiece of the experience is The Orb, an interactive status indicator that shifts appearance based on your data. It provides immediate, non-verbal feedback on your day, but it also serves as a vital circuit breaker for sudden urges. By long-pressing the Orb, you trigger a guided 4-7-8 breathing exercise. Accompanied by mindful citations, it offers a quiet, private moment to pause, breathe through a craving, and regain your focus before making a choice.

A Complete, Flexible Tracker—Free Forever

We believe the tools to monitor your health should be accessible and powerful. Unlike other apps that hide basic logging behind a paywall, Relimie’s core tracker is entirely free. This isn't just about counting units of alcohol; it is a full-spectrum tool that tracks the money spent and the calories consumed from every drink. It is also incredibly flexible—you can use standard measurements or fine-tune volumes, strengths (ABV), and prices on the fly to reflect exactly what is in your hand. Whether you are tracking a pint at home or a cocktail at a lounge, you get a pixel-perfect view of the impact on your wallet and your wellness.

Radical Privacy & Context

Relimie emphasizes that why you drink is just as important as what you drink. Use our integrated Diary and Tagging to identify stressors or physical consequences like poor sleep. Your habits are your business: Relimie is built on a foundation of Offline-First security. No accounts, no emails, and no cloud databases. Everything stays physically on your device. You retain total ownership, with the ability to export your story as a professional PDF report or a full data backup whenever you choose.

Transparent & Fair

The foundational logging, the Orb, and the breathing exercises are perpetually free. To help you build a habit, we provide a usage-based 30-day trial of our advanced analytics and historical diary so you can experience the full power of Relimie’s deep insights at your own pace.
