Partner With Relimie

Relimie is open to working with anyone who wants to help more people drink more mindfully. If you believe in what we're building, we'd love to team up and get Relimie into the hands of the people it can help.

Who we're looking for

  • Influencers & creators in the alcohol-reduction, sober-curious, mindful-drinking, and wellness space. If your audience is rethinking its relationship with alcohol, Relimie is a natural fit.
  • Everyday advocates. You don't need a big following. If Relimie has helped you and you'd like to recommend it to your friends, family, or community, we'd love to support that too.
  • Organizations & professionals — coaches, therapists, and communities focused on healthier drinking habits.

Why partner with us

Relimie is a privacy-first, mindful drinking companion: no accounts, no cloud, no tracking, and a genuinely free core. It's built to help people understand and ease down their drinking at their own pace, without guilt or pressure. That's a message worth sharing, and we're glad to discuss how we can best support your work.

Get in touch

Tell us a little about yourself and how you'd like to collaborate, and we'll get back to you.
