Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Relimie ("the App", "we", "us") handles your data. Your privacy is important to us. Relimie is designed with a privacy-first approach — all personal data remains on your device.

2. Data Collection and Storage

Relimie does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

  • Local Storage Only: All data you enter — including drink logs, diary entries, moods, tags, and personal settings — is stored exclusively on your device.
  • No Accounts Required: Relimie does not require user registration or account creation.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. No behavioral data is collected.

3. Data You Provide

The App allows you to enter the following types of data, all stored locally:

  • Drink consumption logs (type, amount, date/time)
  • Diary entries and mood reflections
  • Custom tags and personal notes
  • App preferences and settings

4. Third-Party Services

Relimie uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may process anonymous transaction data as required by Apple App Store and Google Play Store. RevenueCat's privacy policy can be found at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Subscription purchases are processed through the respective platform. Their privacy policies apply to those transactions.
  • Google Firebase & Google Gemini: Used to provide the AI-assisted drink logging feature. When you use this optional feature, the natural language description of your drink and your ISO country code are transmitted to a secure Firebase Cloud Function (hosted in the EU-Frankfurt) for processing by the Gemini API.

No personal data from the App is shared with these services beyond what is required for subscription management.

5. AI Data Processing

AI-Assisted Drink Logging

Relimie includes an optional AI utility to help you turn text descriptions into structured data. To protect your privacy, this feature follows these strict rules:

  • Data Minimization: We do not send any personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, account details, or precise locations, to the AI model. Only the text you type (e.g., "one glass of red wine") and your country code are processed.
  • No Data Retention: The drink descriptions are processed in real-time. Neither Relimie nor Google stores these descriptions on external servers after the structured data is returned to your device.
  • No Model Training: The data transmitted for drink logging is not used by Google to train or improve its AI models.
  • Local Finality: Once the AI returns the calculated volume and alcohol content, that data is saved exclusively on your local device.

6. Data Export

You may export your data at any time through the App's export function. The exported data remains under your full control.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete all app data at any time through the App's settings.
  • Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.
  • We have no ability to access or recover your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Relimie is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App with an updated "Last updated" date.
