1. Purpose of the Application

The Relimie application is intended for tracking personal alcohol consumption, identifying emotional triggers, and habit reflection. All generated statistics, trend analyses, and habit charts are created automatically based on user-inputted data. They do not constitute scientifically grounded predictions or absolute facts.

2. Not Professional Advice or Diagnosis

The materials and data provided by the application do not replace professional advice or treatment.

  • Not Medical or Psychological Advice: The application does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment for addiction, or therapeutic services. If you have health problems or severe physical dependency, please consult qualified medical professionals.
  • Tracking Tool Only: Relimie is a data-tracking tool designed for mindful reflection, not a medical device for clinical diagnosis.

3. Subscription & Soft Trial Model

Relimie operates on a usage-based freemium model.

  • Free Access: Users have perpetual access to standard drink logging, "The Orb" status indicator, and breathing exercises.
  • Diary History: Historical logs in the Diary remain readable and available to view for all users, regardless of subscription status.
  • 30-Day Limit: After 30 individual days of logging data, the ability to add new diary entries, create custom drinks, or view advanced analytics (Trends/Habits) is restricted.
  • Premium Access: Accessing advanced analytics and the ability to add new diary entries after the trial requires a "Relimie Premium" subscription or a Lifetime purchase.

Premium Options

Users who choose to upgrade have the following purchase options:

  • Lifetime Premium — A one-time payment of €69.99 (EUR for European users) or $69.99 (USD for all other regions) that grants unlimited premium access with no recurring charges.
  • Annual Subscription — Billed once per year at a discounted rate compared to monthly billing.
  • Monthly Subscription — Billed every month, ideal for short-term commitment.

Note on pricing: Prices are displayed in the currency of your region. European users are charged in Euro (€); all other users are charged in US Dollars ($). Actual prices may vary slightly based on local App Store / Google Play pricing in your country.
