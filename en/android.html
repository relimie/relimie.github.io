Test Relimie on Android

Relimie is on its way to the Google Play Store, and you can help it get there.

Before a new app can be released publicly on Google Play, Google requires a period of closed testing: at least 12 testers need to try the app for 14 days. You can read Google's own explanation of this requirement here. By joining the test you genuinely help bring Relimie to Android for everyone.

How to join the test

Please follow these three steps in order, using the same Google account on an Android device:

  1. Request to join our testers group. Open groups.google.com/g/relimie-test and ask to join. We approve requests manually, so it may take a little while.
  2. Become a tester. Once you're approved, open the tester opt-in link and accept the invitation to test.
  3. Install Relimie. Open Relimie on Google Play and install it like any other app.

If the Play Store doesn't show the app right away, give it a little time after joining and make sure you're signed in with the account you used to join the group.

Thank you

Testing makes a real difference, and every tester counts.

🎁 Our thank-you: Everyone who helps test will get a free year of Relimie Premium once the app launches on Android.

If you run into anything or have feedback, write to us at support@relimie.com. Thank you for helping shape Relimie on Android!
