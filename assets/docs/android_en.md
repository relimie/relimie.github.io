# Test Relimie on Android

Relimie is on its way to the Google Play Store, and you can help it get there.

Before a new app can be released publicly on Google Play, Google requires a period of **closed testing**: at least 12 testers need to try the app for 14 days. You can read Google's own explanation of this requirement [here](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/14151465). By joining the test you genuinely help bring Relimie to Android for everyone.

## How to join the test

Please follow these three steps in order, using the **same Google account** on an **Android device**:

1. **Request to join our testers group.** Open [groups.google.com/g/relimie-test](https://groups.google.com/g/relimie-test) and ask to join. We approve requests manually, so it may take a little while.
2. **Become a tester.** Once you're approved, open the [tester opt-in link](https://play.google.com/apps/testing/com.ikaengel.relimie) and accept the invitation to test.
3. **Install Relimie.** Open [Relimie on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ikaengel.relimie) and install it like any other app.

If the Play Store doesn't show the app right away, give it a little time after joining and make sure you're signed in with the account you used to join the group.
