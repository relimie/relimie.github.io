# Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I use the baseline?
You can track your baseline in the **Trends** section of the [Analytics Hub](guide.html#5-analytics-hub). There, you will see your "Target Progress," which calculates exactly how much sober buffer you have left or how many sober days you need to return to your habit baseline.

### How can The Orb help me?
If you feel a sudden craving or urge to drink, **Long-Press The Orb** on the [Home Screen](guide.html#1-personal-baseline). This triggers a mindful 4-7-8 breathing exercise accompanied by inspirational quotes, acting as a mental circuit breaker to help you calm down and reduce sudden cravings.

### How do I log drinks for past days?
For recent days, use the horizontal **date picker** on the [Main Screen](guide.html#1-personal-baseline) by swiping left. To log a sober day, simply tap on The Orb. To log drinks, use the "Add Drink" button. For dates farther in the past, navigate to the **Analytics Hub** and tap on the [Chronological Heatmap](guide.html#5-analytics-hub) to open a date selector.

### How do I enter or adjust my spending?
You have two ways to adjust prices. To change the standard price of a specific drink globally, **long-press the drink card** in the catalog. If your total for the day doesn't match the automatic sum (e.g., due to a discount or shared bill), you can **long-press the Total Price** in the bottom summary bar on the [Logger Screen](guide.html#2-drinks-logging) to manually adjust the final daily amount.
