# Video Guides

Short video tutorials to help you get the most out of Relimie.

### How to set your baseline
Walk through setting your Personal Baseline for the first time, using the country and gender reference values as a starting point.
[Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/gF8Q6OKFtdo)

### How to use the baseline
See how the baseline trend works day to day and how to read the colored Orb to stay on track and reduce your drinking over time.
[Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/GvLLyXe_Pvg)

### How to use the Orb
A quick tour of the Orb on the Home Screen: tap to check your baseline, log a sober day, and long-press for the Cravings Breaker.
[Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/ESJEFKyLymk)

### How to log drinks and spending
Learn how to log a drink, track calories and spending, and use the AI button to add drinks that aren't in the catalog.
[Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/RavzoXUfqvI)

### How to access your analytics pages
Find your way around the Analytics Hub: history, habits, trends, and the full-screen consumption chart.
[Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/d_nndGEZcTU)
