# Website Privacy
Effective Date: April 27, 2026

This page informs you about the privacy practices and the use of cookies/local storage specifically for the Relimie Website (relimie.com). For information regarding our mobile application, please refer to the [Mobile App Privacy Policy](privacy_policy_en.html).

## Cookies and Local Storage
We believe in absolute transparency. Our website does not use tracking, advertising, or marketing cookies. We use minimal technical solutions solely to provide the functionality you expect:

*   **Language Preference (Local Storage):** To remember if you prefer the site in English, German, or Russian so you don't have to select it every time.
*   **Privacy Banner (Local Storage):** To remember that you have acknowledged our privacy notice so it doesn't interrupt your experience again.

## Data Collection
The website itself does not collect any personal data automatically. We do not use analytics services like Google Analytics that track your behavior across the web.

## Your Rights
Since we do not store personal data on our servers, there is no technical way for us to identify or export your data. You can clear all data stored by this website at any time by clearing your browser's cache and site data.
