## For Anyone Who Drinks and Wants to Stay in Control

Relimie is not a sobriety app. It's a mindful drinking companion — built for people who enjoy alcohol socially but want to stay aware of their habits without guilt or judgment.

At the heart of Relimie is your **Personal Baseline** — a long-term orientation target, not a strict daily limit. Think of it as a monthly average you aim to stay around. Some evenings you'll drink a little more, others less or not at all — that's life. What matters is that over time, your consumption gravitates back toward your baseline.

Your glowing **Orb** tells you where you stand at a glance:

- **Green** — you're safely within your baseline
- **Gold** — you're approaching your limit
- **Red** — a recovery day helps bring your average back in line

Your baseline is calibrated to **WHO health guidelines**, adjusted for your country and gender. As your habits improve, you can tighten it — your ceiling should grow stricter as you grow stronger.
