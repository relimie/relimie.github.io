## Drink on Your Own Terms

Relimie isn't about quitting; it's about control. It's a companion for those who enjoy a drink but want to keep their habits from slipping into autopilot — without the guilt or judgment that often comes with tracking.

Everything revolves around your **Personal Baseline**. Instead of a rigid daily limit that feels like a failure if you miss it, your baseline is a long-term anchor. Life happens: some nights you'll have an extra glass, others you won't drink at all. What matters is the trend. Over time, your habits naturally gravitate back toward your goal.

Your glowing **Orb** shows you where you stand at a glance:

- **Green** — you're comfortably within your baseline
- **Gold** — you're approaching your limit
- **Red** — a recovery day will help bring your average back in line

Your baseline is calibrated to **WHO health guidelines**, adjusted for your country and gender. As you find your balance, you can tighten it — your ceiling should grow stricter as you grow stronger.
